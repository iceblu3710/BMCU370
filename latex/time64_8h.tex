Get 64-\/bit System Time in Milliseconds. 

Handles rollover of the 32-\/bit {\ttfamily millis()} counter to provide a continuous 64-\/bit time. Must be called frequently (at least once every 49 days) to detect rollover.

\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
uint64\+\_\+t Time in ms.
