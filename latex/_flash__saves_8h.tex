Save a buffer to Flash Memory. 

Erases the required number of pages starting at {\ttfamily address} and programs the data. Reloads IWDG during the process to prevent watchdog reset.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}

\hline

\hline
{\em address} & Destination Flash address (Page Aligned recommended). \\
